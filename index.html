About Us

Sevo Certified Translation and Languages, based in Cairo, Egypt, is a fast growing translation agency. We translate more than 120 language combinations for clients all over the world. We are certified under the supervision of Arab Federation for Translators (ARBFT). For more than 13 years, we have translated, proofread and edited hundreds of thousands of documents in different fields (e.g. medical, legal, financial, literal, technical, localization, general) for high profile companies all over the world. We care about quality as much as about confidentiality. Our network of translators has proved proficiency in all fields. We assign each translation task to the most professional native in the field. Hence, we guarantee you workflow.
